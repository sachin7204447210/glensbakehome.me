About Glen's Bake House

Glen's Bake House founded in 2013 at Indiranagar, conceptualizes a hot kitchen and live bakery under the same roof. On offer is a variety of freshly prepared Italian and Continental cuisine, cakes and savories, complemented with a wide range of desserts and non-alcoholic drinks. The ambience at Glen's has been designed to maximize customer comfort, giving a choice of both indoor and outdoor seating.
